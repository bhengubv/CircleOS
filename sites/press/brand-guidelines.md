# Circle OS Brand Guidelines

## Logo

The Circle OS logo is the ◯ symbol (Unicode U+25EF, Large Circle) followed by "Circle OS" in Inter Bold.

**Symbol:** ◯
**Wordmark:** ◯ Circle OS
**Colour (on dark):** #00D4FF (Circle Cyan)
**Colour (on light):** #0D0D1A (Circle Dark)
**Font:** Inter Bold, letter-spacing -0.02em

Do not use the logo on backgrounds that make it unreadable. Minimum clear space: 16px on all sides.

## Typography

**Body:** Inter (Google Fonts), Regular 400, SemiBold 600, Bold 700, ExtraBold 800
**Code:** JetBrains Mono

Font stack: `'Inter', system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif`

---

## Voice (quick reference)

- **Direct.** "Your phone isn't old. Your OS is."
- **Confident.** "Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation."
- **Warm.** "Built for load shedding, expensive data, and real life."
- **African.** "Made in South Africa. Made for you."

Do not say: "solutions", "leverage", "synergy", "best-in-class", "next-generation".

---

## Press use

The Circle OS name, logo, and brand assets may be used in press coverage without prior permission, provided:
- They are used accurately and not in a misleading context
- The OS is not misrepresented as commercial or proprietary

For all other uses, contact info@thegeek.co.za.
