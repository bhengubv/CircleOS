The problem

No signal? No internet? Traditional phones are useless without infrastructure.

The solution

Circle OS devices form a mesh network. Messages hop from phone to phone until they arrive. No tower needed.

How it works

Every Circle OS device is a node. When you send a message, nearby devices relay it. One person with internet connects everyone.

Benefits

  • Works offline
  • Community-powered
  • Encrypted end-to-end
  • Shares OS updates too
Technical details

WiFi Direct. Bluetooth LE. mDNS. Store-and-forward. 5-hop TTL. ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption.
