⇄ Dual Boot

Keep your banking apps. Gain everything else.

The problem

Android or iOS — you have to choose. Privacy or your banking apps. Not both.

The solution

Circle OS supports dual boot. Slot A keeps original Android. Slot B is Circle OS. Reboot to switch. Zero risk.

How it works

Your phone gets two slots. Slot A has everything familiar — banking, work, Google. Slot B has Circle OS — private, fast, offline-ready.

Benefits

  • No compromises
  • Zero risk
  • Instant switching
  • Both worlds
Technical details

A/B partition scheme. Separate storage per slot. No cross-slot access.
