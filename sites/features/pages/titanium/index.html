Tap to pay. Even without internet.

The problem

Digital payments need internet. When the network is down or data is expensive, you are stuck.

The solution

SDPKT Titanium is a hardware-grade wallet built into Circle OS. Tap phone-to-phone to send Shongololo. No internet needed.

How it works

Your phone becomes a wallet. Tap another Circle OS phone to send or receive. Transactions sync when you are back online.

Benefits

  • Offline (NFC tap-to-pay)
  • Secure (TEE hardware protection)
  • Protected (stress detection + location rules)
  • Free (no transaction fees)
Technical details

Hardware keystore. Passive duress mode. Mesh settlement queue. SDPKT protocol.
